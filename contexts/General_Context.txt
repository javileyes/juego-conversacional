The story takes place in the Moreau Mansion, an old and majestic property located on the outskirts of the city. The patriarch of the Moreau family, Bernard Moreau, has been found dead in his study under mysterious circumstances. The door was locked from the inside and there were no obvious signs of violence or struggle. Police initially ruled it a suicide, but some details don't add up and the family has insisted on a deeper investigation. Your character, Detective Alex Reynard, has been called in to investigate the death.
